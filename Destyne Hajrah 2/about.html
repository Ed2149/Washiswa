About Me

I’m Hajrah Washiswa, a creative artist, writer, and designer with a passion for storytelling and visual expression. I bring words, graphics, and experiences to life using digital tools and emotion. Inspired by everyday life, nature, and Kenyan culture — I share moments through poems, essays, photos, and visuals.

My journey started with curiosity. Despite limited resources, I taught myself how to design and write. Today, I keep growing through feedback, practice, and creative exploration.

Vision: To inspire others and make social impact through creativity.
